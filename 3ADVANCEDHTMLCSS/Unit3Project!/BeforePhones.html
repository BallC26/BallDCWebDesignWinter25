Before the Introduction of the Smartphone

Before mobile phones and especially smartphones, people worked with fewer distractions. Communication was more intentional, and employees typically focused on tasks without constant interruptions from calls, texts, or notifications.

How Was Productivity Before Smartphones

Before smartphones, people were more productive and did more work. Smartphones offer distractions from work and lower productivity. It was much higher with much more work being done in a day and mostly all work being accomplished without distractions.

Social Interactions Before Smartphones

People were more interactive with eachother and talked to eachother more instead of being attached to a screen all day. Before their Introduction, phones were only made for calls to friends, family, or coworkers.
