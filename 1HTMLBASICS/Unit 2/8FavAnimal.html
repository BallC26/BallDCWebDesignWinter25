The Parrot

parrot is one of the smartest birds in the world. Having some of the highest cognative abilites other than crows and ravens, they are able to solve puzzles using "tools" and other objects to help them acheive their goal. They can learn to speak fluently in languages, and be taught to do tricks. Their bright colors set them apart from other birds and helps them to blend into their tropical environment as well as attract females for mating. These smart birds are found native to south america and sometimes southern United States. Many are kept as pets or found in zoos.
