Facts about Birds

One of my favorite parts of a bird is it's colors. Every bird has bright different colors and every different species has its own pattern of colors. Birds have many different variations of feathers for many reasons. A few could be Mating with a partner, Showing territory, communicating with other birds and hiding from predators. My favorite species of bird is the parrot. Known for their bright colors and smarts. Parrots can be found in a few places in the US as well as anywhere hot and tropical. Parrots can be found in places such as Texas, Florida, and San Fransisco mostly for their semi tropical environments and abundant fruit trees.


Another thing I love about Birds is how smart they are. Birds are considered to be among the most intelligent animals on planet Earth. Corvids (Crows, Jays, Magpies) and psittacines (Parrots, Cockatoos, Macaws) are considered to be the most intelligent type of birds, having high problem solving skills and cognative abilities. They're known to use "tools" to solve puzzles. Bent pieces of metal rods or certain shaped sticks to try and grab their food from places they can't reach. Birds are so intelligent because of the high density of neurons in their brains allowing for complex cognative abilities. They have a similar brain structure to the mammalian prefrontal cortex which is very important for planning and sensory systems. This helps them navigate problems in their environment.
